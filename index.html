Commercial Equipment Brand Distribution

Following a recent business conversation, I explored how publicly available spatial data could be used to visualize the geographic distribution of commercial equipment brands.

This proof-of-concept demonstrates how data analysis combined with location intelligence can help reveal market presence, regional patterns, and potential business opportunities.

The project uses OpenStreetMap data collected through Overpass Turbo, processed in QGIS, and prepared for web-based visualization.
